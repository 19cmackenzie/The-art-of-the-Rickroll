The art of the Rickroll

Origin

The 'Rickroll' is an internet meme originating from the popular song "Never Gonna Give You Up" by the musical artist Rick Astley. Upon release to the internet, users of the 4chan social site would prank others by hyperlinking the music video when the recipient believes they are accessing unrelated material. Victims of the popular prank are said to have been 'rickrolled'

How to sucessfully pull off a rickroll

Pulling off a simple rickroll is easy, but there can be an art to the execution. There are stories around the internet of interesting ways people have been rickrolled
Some have come across QR codes on murals around their cities. Others have been tricked by links in youtube videos.
